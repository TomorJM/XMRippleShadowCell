### How to Use it?

#### 1. 

Drag `XMRippleShadowCell.h` and `XMRippleShadowCell.m` to your progect.

#### 2. 

Creat your custom cell which is subclassed from `XMRippleShadowCell`.

#### 3、

Use your custom cell as usual.
